Now it will ask to do :("git rm -r --cached 'data'" and "git commit -m stop tracking data" )
because inititally we were tracking data/folder from git now  we remove it for DVC to handle.

8>.Again we do "py -m dvc add data/" (creates data.dvc) then "git add.gitignore data.dvc"
